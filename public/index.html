Brazilian forró classes & events in the heart of Liverpool

Beginner-friendly, Roots-style forró dance classes and live music events in the heart of Liverpool. No partner needed. Come try a free intro class!

Come dance with us

Dance classes, live music events, and Brazilian cultural events in Liverpool, England. Teachers from Recife teach a mix of styles (pé de serra, universitário, with a roots focus) to all-levels students. We regularly host guest teachers like Luis Henrique, Mardio Costa & Milena Morais, and Lucas Dumont.
